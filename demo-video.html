The Workflow

Four A2A Agents Working Together

A2A Agent Orchestration
User: "Screen this patient for SDOH needs"
sdoh_bridge_agent (port 3001)
Reads FHIR patient record
Interprets SDOH screening responses
→ Z59.6, Z58.2, Z58.5, Z59.1
resource_agent (port 3002)
Matches Z-codes to community resources
→ Community Food Bank, Hope Housing
referral_agent (port 3003)
Generates formatted referral packet
→ Referral letter + FHIR write-back
outreach_agent (port 3004)
Schedules 14-day follow-up
→ Task created in Prompt Opinion
